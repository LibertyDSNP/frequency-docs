# Frequency Style Guide

Style guide defining wording, spelling, and other details regarding how Frequency terms are referenced.

For more information about Frequency, visit [frequency.xyz](https://www.frequency.xyz) 
or [Frequency on GitHub](https://github.com/LibertyDSNP/frequency)



## Activity Content Types
Uppercase A, C and T

## Announcement
Uppercase A

## Announcement Type
Uppercase A and T

## Avro Type
Uppercase A and T

## Batch
Uppercase B

## Batch Publication
Uppercase B and P

## Capacity
Uppercase C, always add “currently under development”

## Capacity Economic System
Uppercase C, E and S 

## Capacity Token Rewards
Uppercase C, T and R

## Code of Conduct
Uppercase C and C

## Collator Rewards
Uppercase C and R

## Collator 
Uppercase C

## community
All lowercase

## Content IDentifier
Uppercase C, I, and D

## Contribution Guidelines
Uppercase C and G

## delegate
Used as a verb ONLY i.e. an MSA delegates to a Provider, not as a noun

## development community
All lowercase

## DSNP public social graph
All lowercase after acronym

## DSNP
Just DSNP (no the before it and never DSNP protocol)

## DSNP Batch Publications
Uppercase Acronym plus uppercase B and P

## DSNP Content Hash
Uppercase Acronym plus uppercase C and H

## DSNP graph
Only acronym is uppercase

## DSNP Identifiers
Uppercase Acronym plus uppercase I

## DSNP Identity
Uppercase Acronym plus Uppercase I

## DSNP Id
Short for DSNP Identity, Uppercase Acronym plus uppercase I

## DSNP ID
Short for DSNP Identity Document, do not confuse with DSNP Id

## DSNP Protocol Scheme
Uppercase Acronym plus uppercase P and S

## DSNP Specification
Uppercase DSNP and S, avoid preceding with the word "the"

## DSNP User Id
Uppercase acronym, U and I, avoid preceding with the word "the"

## DSNP User URI
Uppercase Acronyms plus uppercase U

## End User
Uppercase E and U

##  Exclamation Marks
Should be avoided

## Field Names
Use Camel Caps i.e. announcementType, contentHash

## Frequency
Uppercase F, avoid preceding with the word "the"

## Frequency Council
Uppercase F and C

## Frequency Message
Uppercase F and M

## FRQCY 
Avoid following with the word "tokens"

## Generated Capacity
Uppercase G and C

## Graph Change Announcements
Uppercase G, C and A

## Graph Change event
Uppercase G and C lowercase e

## InterPlanetary File System (IPFS)
Uppercase I, P, F and S. In the first instance, spell out followed by the acronym in parentheses. Use the acronym only thereafter.

## Maximum Capacity Staking
Uppercase M, C and S

## Message Source Account (MSA)
Uppercase M, S and A. In the first instance, spell out followed by the acronym in parentheses. Use the acronym only thereafter. Never MSA Account.

## Messages
Uppercase M

## MUST/MUST NOT
All Caps when defining rules for specification

## Oxford Comma
Always use

## parachain
All lowercase

## Parquet
Uppercase P (proper noun)

## Parquet Schema
Uppercase P and S

## Parquet Type
Uppercase P and T

## Polkadot
Uppercase P

## Polkadot accounts
Uppercase P

## Polkadot Relay Chain
Polkadot wiki uses uppercase R and C

## Possessives
Making Frequency or DSNP possessive should be avoided (e.g. Frequency’s or DSNP’s)

## Profile Activity Type
Uppercase P, A and T

## Project Liberty
Uppercase P and L

## Providers
Uppercase P

## public graph
All lowercase

## Reaction Announcement
Uppercase R and A

## Rewards Capacity Staking
Uppercase R, C and S

## Roadmap
Uppercase R

## Schema
Uppercase S

## Schema Models
Uppercase S and M

## Schema Registry
Uppercase S and R

## Schema Id
One word uppercase S and I

## Session Key
Uppercase S and K

## social network graph
All lowercase

## Substrate
Uppercase S

## Team
Should not be used in context with Frequency

## Technical Committee
Uppercase T and C

## Token Account
Uppercase T and A

## Update Announcement
Uppercase U and A

## We/Us/Our
Should not be used in context with Frequency

## XRQCY
All uppercase, avoid following with the word "token"
